Hello, my name is Nicholos Palmer. I enjoy threading words together to create impactful and inspiring stories. In my writing, I aim to sew in the lived experiences and voice of my clients.

Along with this, I attempt to balance his/her/their nuanced informalities and conventional idiosyncrasies to paint authentic stories and accounts.

In other words, my goal in writing is to prioritize the distinctive aspect of my individual.
